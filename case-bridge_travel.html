Bridge.Travel Application

Mobile Application

Role Product Designer
Product release July, 2024

Bridge.Travel is a service for reliable and fast parcel delivery worldwide. Previously, users placed orders through a Telegram channel, but the company decided to develop a cross-platform app for iOS and Android to enhance the user experience, scale the business, and automate processes. The app allows users to conveniently find travelers willing to carry a parcel to the desired country, track shipment statuses, and safely interact with other users. The main challenge of the project was to create an intuitive interface that maintains the simplicity and convenience of the service that users were already familiar with through the messenger.
