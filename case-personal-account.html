The participant's exhibition Account

B2B Web Platform

Role Product Designer
Product release September, 2023

This project involved the creation of a visitor's personal account design tailored for a construction equipment exhibition. The design was meticulously crafted to reflect the exhibition's corporate color scheme and optimized for mobile devices, ensuring that exhibition participants could easily access the information they needed and make updates from their mobile devices. The primary goal of this project was to provide a user-friendly and efficient platform for participants. To achieve this, I focused on simplicity and minimalism in the design, emphasizing ease of use and accessibility.
